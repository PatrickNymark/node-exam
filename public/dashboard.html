Game Rules

Placement of bets

How to get started: To get started, please add deposit to your account.

How to place a bet: To place a bet, be sure to have a positive balance on your user, and pick atleast 1 bet from the market place. For each bet on the coupon the accumulated value for each bet is multiplied, if the coupon is in compliance with the rules. In case of winning, your total deposit is multiplied by the total odds. In case of losing, your total deposit is lost.

How to win a bet: All bets on the coupon has to be guessed correct to win. Once a game is finished, the bet will either turn won/lost based on the choice.
